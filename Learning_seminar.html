Informal Learning Seminar on Quantum Groups and Related Topics

トップページ, Top

This is an informal learning seminar on quantum groups and related topics, mainly held at Tokyo Institute of Technology. Please feel free to contact me (Hironori OYA) if you would like to participate.

Seminar talks are given mainly in Japanese, while some notes are written in English.
